schedule+

2022-06-15

Switching lighting flexibly via Web App


schedule+ enables time-controlled switching of devices simply with the help of a web/browser dialog on the smartphone, tablet or PC. schedule+ supports WLAN/Tasmota based devices such as Sonoff.

The browser is used not only for daily operation, but also for settings, log file output, and help/documentation.

schedule+ is an excellent tool to switch the lights of the apartment/house even in absence. This simulates activities in the house. A daily schedule can change the switching times with sunrise/sunset and also all times can be varied by random values.
